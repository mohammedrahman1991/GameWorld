Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Wacky Brains (wackybrains.com) uses them, and the choices you have regarding cookies. By continuing to use our site, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners about how their site is being used.

Cookies are not programs and cannot carry viruses or install malicious software on your device. They cannot access any other data on your computer.

3. Google AdSense & Advertising Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google AdSense uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and/or other sites on the internet. These cookies allow Google and its partners to serve ads that may be relevant to you based on your browsing activity.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it to serve ads based on:

  • Your visits to Wacky Brains and other sites on the internet.
  • Your demographic information and inferred interests.
  • The content of the pages you visit.

You can opt out of personalized Google advertising at any time by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Site functionality — Ensuring pages load correctly and games run smoothly.
  • Usage analytics — Understanding which games are most popular and how players navigate the site, so we can improve the experience.
  • Advertising revenue — Wacky Brains is a free platform. Advertising helps cover our hosting and development costs.
  • User preferences — Remembering any settings you've chosen during your visit.

5. How to Control or Delete Cookies

You have the right to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline them if you prefer. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of Wacky Brains and other websites.

Browser cookie controls:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
💡 Tip: You can use your browser's Private / Incognito mode to browse without cookies being stored on your device for that session.

Opt out of interest-based advertising:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
