Last updated: April 12, 2026

Welcome to Wacky Brains ("we", "us", or "our"), accessible at wackybrains.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site and play our games.

We do not require registration or accounts. We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email, or address. However, third-party services we use may collect data automatically:

  • IP address and general location

We and our advertising partners use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate the site and serve relevant ads. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though doing so may affect site functionality.

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other sites on the internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may also opt out via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

For more information on how Google uses data, visit: How Google uses data from sites that use Google services.

4. Children's Privacy

Some games on Wacky Brains are designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our site complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Parents and guardians should be aware that Google AdSense may serve ads on our site. We recommend supervising children's internet use.
