Frequently Asked Questions

Are the games really free?
Yes — every single game on Wacky Brains is 100% free to play, forever. No hidden subscriptions, no pay-to-win mechanics, no premium unlocks.

Do I need to create an account?
No account is ever required. Just visit the site and click play. We don't store any personal information about your game sessions.

Can I suggest a new game?
Absolutely! We love hearing game ideas from our community. Use the form above or email us directly with "Game Idea" in the subject line.

I found a bug — how do I report it?
Use the contact form above and select "Bug Report" as the topic. Please include the game name, what happened, and what browser/device you were using. Screenshots help a lot!

Can I embed your games on my site?
Please contact us first before embedding. We review embedding requests case by case to ensure a good experience for all players.
