A — Split Keyboard (WASD + Arrows)

Player 1 uses WASD + nearby keys. Player 2 uses Arrow keys + numpad/punctuation. Both play on same keyboard simultaneously.

ONE PLAYER AT A TIME (TAKE TURNS)
← → move
↑ jump
SPACE shoot
Z dribble
X pass/steal
AI controls the other player
OR offense/defense swap each possession

B — Single Keyboard + AI Opponent

One human plays, AI controls the other. Simpler controls, more polished solo experience. AI uses player stats to make decisions.

Menu lets you pick mode before tip-off

C — Both Modes (1P vs AI + 2P Split)

Menu lets you choose: play solo vs AI, or 2-player on same keyboard. Best of both worlds. Slightly more to build.

My recommendation: Option C (Both Modes) — gives you and Noah flexibility to play together or solo. The AI can use Curry/Ant stats to play realistically.
