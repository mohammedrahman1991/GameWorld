How shooting works

  • 🏀 Outside the 3PT arc → hold G (Curry) or / (Ant) → shot meter fills → release in green → 3 points
  • 🏀 Inside the arc → same button → shot meter → 2 points
  • 💥 Edwards in dunk zone → pressing shoot = dunk animation → 2 points, no meter needed
  • 🤸 Anyone at the rim (low speed) → layup animation → 2 points
  • ✨ Curry 3PT → ball arc turns gold, green zone is wider than Ant's
